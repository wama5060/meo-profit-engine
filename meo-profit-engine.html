The Offer Formula
One niche. One channel. One outcome promise.

Bad: "We do AI-powered marketing"
Good: "We book 10 qualified calls/month for e-com brands doing $30K–$200K/mo or you don't pay"

Charge $500–$1,500 setup + $1,000–$2,500/mo retainer. First client should be closeable in weeks 1–2 with consistent outreach.
⚠ Compliance Rules
CAN-SPAM: Honest headers, physical address, unsubscribe link on every email.

FCC: Seller-specific written consent required for texts/robocalls. Do not mass-text without consent.

Keep domain reputation clean — warm up new domains for 2+ weeks before sending volume. One spam complaint flood kills your domain permanently.

Timeline Reality Check
First agency client: Weeks 1–2 with consistent outreach
First store sale: Weeks 2–4
First profitable month: Weeks 6–10 (if you cut losers fast)
Buyer-ready store: 6–12 months minimum

Don't believe the "flip after 2 months" influencer claims. Brokers require documented earning history. Build it right from day one.
⚠ The 4 Risks That Kill New Stores
1. Shipping claims — FTC requires honest timelines. Lying = frozen store.
2. Chargebacks — Stripe flags at ~1%. Visa threshold 150bps. One bad week can kill your processor.
3. Cold email compliance — CAN-SPAM + FCC. One spam flood kills your domain forever.
4. No financial tracking — Most stores die from cash flow blindness, not bad products.
